Join QuRAC

Opportunities to work with Dr. Laltu Sardar and his QuRAC Research Group at IISER Thiruvananthapuram.

We are looking for motivated individuals interested in applied cryptography, post-quantum Cloud Security. We offer a rigorous, collaborative research environment at IISER Thiruvananthapuram with strong connections to the international cryptographic research community.

Note: Applicants focused only on AI/ML and not interested in security research are encouraged not to apply. All positions require on-campus presence. Only shortlisted candidates will be notified. For the most current information, visit the official open positions page.

Ph.D. in Cryptography

Candidates motivated to pursue a Ph.D. in Applied Cryptography and Computer Security, focusing on Quantum-Safe Cloud Computing (including emerging Post-Quantum Cryptography), a high-demand field with strong market relevance and excellent career opportunities.

Preferred Background

  • Candidate from Mathematics with strong foundations in algebra, number theory, and probability.
  • Candidate from Computer Science with strong knowledge of algorithms, data structures, and programming in C.

Eligibility

  • Candidates must have qualified in a national-level examination in the relevant subject.
  • Master's degree (M.Sc., M.S., M.Tech., etc.) in Mathematics, Computer Science, Engineering, or related areas — highly preferred.
  • Bachelor's degree (B.E./B.Tech.) in CS, IT, ECE, or related disciplines — also eligible.
  • Prior knowledge of cryptography is not mandatory.
  • For other eligibility criteria (age limits, minimum marks, etc.) please refer to the latest advertisement.

Application Process

Regular Route — Apply Online  ⚠️ Wait for the winter cycle

Regular Ph.D. Admission (via IISER TVM Portal)

Candidates without any fellowship must apply online through the IISER Thiruvananthapuram Ph.D. admission portal. Such positions may be supported through institute funding. Shortlisted candidates will be invited for online interviews as per institutional policy.
